Detailed Description

Schroeder tone complex class.

The Schroeder tone complex is a sweep defined in the sampled spectrum:

is the sampled frequency in Hz, is the sign of the sweep (-1 for up sweep, +1 for down sweep), is the sweep duration in samples, is the sampling rate in Hz and is the relative sweep speed.

Construct create Schroeder tone complex from a given frequency function.

The frequency function defines the sweep speed and sign (based on the group delay). It must be defined in the interval [0,1) and should return values in the interval [0,1].

Parameters
lenLength of the Schroeder tone complex in samples.
channelsNumber of channels.
freqfunFrequency function .
fminStart frequency (relative to Nyquist frequency).
fmaxEnd frequency (relative to Nyquist frequency).
